A Friendly Kickoff

March 20-23, 2014

I recently started supporting a company as a user Experience Designer and Front End Developer on an Agile development team. It's been an incredible experience thus far where I'm learning a lot and doing what I absolutely love. It wasn't long before I started bonding with my teammates. One of them is an exceptionally talented .NET developer. He's not only skilled with code but he's a strong leader and is eager to educate those around him. I've learned a ton and am very grateful for it.

One evening as I was checking my work email I saw a note from Daniel. He asked is I have ever heard of Jekyll. I sure had. I've been working with CMSs for years and also am a HUGE fan of the Shoptalkshow with Dave Rupert and Chris Coyier. I know that Dave is really enjoying Jekyll and I had played around with it a bit myself, especially after reading Anna Debenham's 24 Ways article about GitHub Pages that included a bonus Jekyll section.

It's now a few days later and we are getting things started. Below I'll post links to assets, patterns and other tidbits as I go along.
